THIS WEEK IN F1: 10 quiz questions on all the F1 news after the inaugural Las Vegas Grand Prix

11月 22, 2023
  • B!

Have you caught your breath yet after that thriller of a Grand Prix under the neon lights of Las Vegas?

Max Verstappen may have notched up another win - his 18th of the season no less - but it was a far from straightforward victory for the Dutchman who had to overcome a five second penalty, two Safety Car periods, and challenges from Charles Leclerc and Sergio Perez in order to cross the line first.

There was also plenty of action further down the field with a spin on Lap 1 for Fernando Alonso, with a gaggle of other drivers also caught up in the chaos.

So there was plenty going on - the question is, were you paying attention?

To find out, we’ve knocked up a quick quiz for you on all the action from Vegas, plus a few questions on next week’s season finale in Abu Dhabi.
