2023年アブダビグランプリの天気予報は？

11月 23, 2023
  • B!

ドライバーとチームはシーズン最終戦アブダビグランプリに向けて準備を進めているが、ヤス・マリーナ・サーキットではどのような気象条件が予想されるだろうか?

The drivers and their teams are preparing for the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix - the final race of the season - but what weather conditions can they expect to be greeted by at the Yas Marina Circuit?

Max Verstappen and Red Bull have already sealed both championships this year but with just one race left on the calendar there is still plenty to decide for both the drivers and the teams.

The battle for P4 in the drivers’ standings is set to go down to the wire with Fernando Alonso, Carlos Sainz, Lando Norris, and Charles Leclerc all separated by just 12 points.

Mercedes are also looking to hold off competition from Ferrari for P2 in the constructors’ championship, while Aston Martin will be hoping to overhaul the 15-point gap to McLaren for P4. Further down the order, Williams will be looking to hold off AlphaTauri, Alfa Romeo, and Haas for seventh.

The radar makes for good reading on all three days, with hot weather and clear skies predicted throughout the weekend. For a much more detailed forecast scroll down.
